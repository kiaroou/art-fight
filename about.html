"Under the Moon" is a interactive website I made for Art Fight! You play as a small sucklet as you explore the festival. Tonight is when vampires and werewolves alike celebrate the blood moon together. There are several attractions and activities to participate in! There are three main places you can visit; The Haunted House, Food Court, and Game Corner. One more thing, try click on anything that seems . . . out of place.
